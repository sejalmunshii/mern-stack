About Us

In May 1997, the Ahmedabad Municipal Corporation launched a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) — the Sabarmati Riverfront Development Corporation Limited (SRFDCL) under Section 149(3) of the Indian Companies Act, 1956.

The project aims to provide Ahmedabad with a meaningful waterfront environment along the banks of the Sabarmati River and to redefine the identity of Ahmedabad around the river.

Nurturing the Environment

Urban forestry is the new thrust area to increase the green cover in the city and enhance livability. The Biodiversity Park and Miyawaki plantations have increased the green cover and include many native and endangered species.

A Paradise for Birds

Sabarmati Riverfront is home to more than 120 species of native and migratory birds including Flamingo, Heron, Egrets, Eagles, Owlets, and many more, making it a major destination for bird lovers.

Commencement of Phase II

Phase II includes stepped promenades, road networks, green parks, children’s play areas, food plazas, and amphitheaters — all aimed at urban regeneration and environmental improvement.
